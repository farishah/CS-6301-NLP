Village River Scene, Bangladesh is a riverine nation situated in the middle of the Ganges delta. Mostly flat, there are a few hilly areas in the south east and the north east., The delta landmass comprise mainly of three mighty rivers the-Ganges, the Brahmaputra and the Meghna, with a network of numerous rivers and canals. Vast green fields are hounded by low hills in the northeast and the southeast with an average elevation of 244 and 610 metres respectively.
